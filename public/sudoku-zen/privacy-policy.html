2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, certain information is collected automatically by our integrated third-party services. We do not directly collect or store this information on our own servers.

a) Usage and Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)

  • Screen views and in-app navigation events
  • In-game events (e.g., puzzle started, puzzle completed, hint used, difficulty selected)
  • Session duration and session frequency
  • App version and build number
  • General device information (device model, operating system version, language/locale)
  • Country or region (derived from IP address at request time; IP address itself is not retained by Firebase after derivation)

This data is collected in an anonymized and aggregated form to help us understand how the App is used and to improve the user experience. No data collected by Firebase Analytics is linked to your name, email address, or any other directly identifying information.

b) Crash and Diagnostics Data (Firebase Crashlytics)

When the App encounters an unexpected error or crash, the following information is automatically sent to Firebase Crashlytics:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android OS version
  • App version and build number
  • Stack traces and error logs at the time of the crash
  • Date and time of the crash event
  • Whether the session was crash-free

No personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or location is collected by Crashlytics.

c) Push Notification Token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

If you grant the App permission to send push notifications (for example, daily puzzle reminders or streak alerts), Firebase Cloud Messaging assigns your device a pseudonymous device registration token. This token:

  • Is used solely to route push notifications to your device
  • Is not linked to your name, email address, or any other personal identifier
  • Can be reset at any time by revoking notification permissions or clearing the App's data

You may opt out of push notifications at any time via your device's system notification settings or within the App's Settings screen.

d) Advertising Identifier and Ad-Related Data (Google AdMob)

The App is supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob. To serve and measure advertisements, AdMob may collect:

  • Google Advertising ID (GAID) — a resettable, pseudonymous identifier provided by the Android platform
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address (used to determine approximate geographic location for ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, ad engagement)
  • Behavioral and interest-based data for personalized advertising (subject to your consent and applicable law)

AdMob's data collection and use are governed by Google's Privacy Policy (see Section 4). You can opt out of personalized advertising by resetting your Advertising ID in your device's Google Settings, or by using the ad controls available at adssettings.google.com.

e) Remote Configuration Data (Firebase Remote Config)

The App fetches configuration values from Firebase Remote Config to enable or adjust features without requiring an app update. This service does not collect any user data or transmit any personal information to our servers.

2.2 Information You Provide (In-App Purchases)

If you choose to unlock premium features via an in-app purchase (Monthly subscription, Yearly subscription, or Lifetime purchase), the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive, handle, or store your payment card details, billing address, or any financial information. Google Play provides us only with a confirmation of the purchase and an associated purchase token to validate your entitlement within the App.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 On-Device Storage

All game progress, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device using Android's DataStore. This data is sandboxed within the App's private storage, is not accessible to other apps, and is deleted permanently if you uninstall the App.

5.2 Third-Party Server Storage

Data collected by Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, FCM) is stored on Google's secure infrastructure. Data collected by AdMob is stored on Google's advertising infrastructure. We do not operate our own servers for the App.

5.3 Security Measures

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Relying on Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure for all cloud-based data
  • Collecting only the minimum data necessary for the App to function
  • Not transmitting local on-device data to external servers
  • Using Android's sandboxed storage for all locally persisted data

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5.4 International Data Transfers

Because we use Firebase and AdMob (both operated by Google LLC, a US-based company), data processed by these services may be transferred to and stored in the United States or other countries outside your jurisdiction. Google implements appropriate safeguards for such transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, in compliance with GDPR Article 46.
